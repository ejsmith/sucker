Sucker! Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Sucker! is a head-to-head dice game. This policy explains what data the app uses to provide accounts, multiplayer games, invites, stats, and notifications.

Information We Collect

When you use multiplayer features, Sucker! uses your email address for sign-in through Supabase Auth. The app also stores your profile name, optional username, game records, invite codes, turn history, score history, head-to-head stats, and push notification tokens for devices where notifications are enabled.

How We Use Information

We use this information to sign you in, show your profile to opponents, create and join games, keep game state synchronized, calculate stats, send turn and game notifications, prevent unauthorized game actions, and improve reliability.

Sharing

Sucker! uses Supabase for authentication, database storage, realtime updates, and Edge Functions. Sucker! uses Expo push notification services to deliver notifications. We do not sell personal information.

Retention

Account, profile, game, and notification data is kept while your account is active or as needed to operate the game, resolve abuse or security issues, comply with legal obligations, and maintain service integrity.

Account Deletion

You can start account deletion from the app by opening Profile, then Delete Account. You can also visit Account Deletion directly.

Contact

For privacy or account questions, contact support@sucker.games.
