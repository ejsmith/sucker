Sucker! Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

Sucker! is a head-to-head dice game. This policy explains what data the app uses to provide accounts, multiplayer games, invites, stats, and notifications.

Information We Collect

When you use multiplayer features, Sucker! uses your email address for sign-in through Supabase Auth. The app also stores your profile name, optional username, optional profile photo, game records, invite codes, turn history, score history, head-to-head stats, and push notification tokens for devices where notifications are enabled. The app also collects error diagnostics through Exceptionless, including app and update versions, device platform, a pseudonymous profile ID, sanitized screen name, error details, and stack traces. Exceptionless private-information collection is disabled. Camera or photo-library access is requested only when you choose to take or select a profile photo.

How We Use Information

We use this information to sign you in, show your profile to opponents, create and join games, keep game state synchronized, calculate stats, send turn and game notifications, prevent unauthorized game actions, and improve reliability.

Sharing

Sucker! uses Supabase for authentication, database storage, realtime updates, and Edge Functions. Sucker! uses Expo push notification services to deliver notifications and Exceptionless for error diagnostics. We do not sell personal information.

Your display name, username, and profile photo are shown to other signed-in players who find or play with you. Profile photos use a public asset URL so they can load during games; anyone who obtains that URL can view the photo. You can replace or remove your profile photo at any time from Profile.

Retention

Account, profile, game, photo, and notification data is kept while your account is active or as needed to operate the game, resolve abuse or security issues, comply with legal obligations, and maintain service integrity. The app removes replaced and deleted profile photos from active storage as part of those actions.

Account Deletion

You can start account deletion from the app by opening Profile, then Delete Account. You can also visit Account Deletion directly.

Contact

For privacy or account questions, contact support@sucker.games.
